FLAG CAPTURE

Domination style game system with light flags and scores panel.

Install a scoring system on your course to create a more competitive and fun experience for your players. The goal is for each of the two teams to capture the maximum number of flags for as long as possible. The more flags a team has, the faster their score will rise.
  • Configurable capture speed
  • Resistant materials (polycarbonate)
  • Long distance wireless communication
  • Supply with power banks (5V) or directly to the electrical network. Battery not included.

    ATTACKABLE RESPAWN

    Use this attackable spawn to add more dynamism to the game. The players will use other areas of the field and the attack front will change. The respawn is attackable and is automatically disabled by receiving one or more hits (configurable) on its green metal box. Once disabled, respawn will be available again after a few minutes (configurable).

    • Light colors (enabled, disabled), impacts, and cooldown times configurable over Wi-Fi
    • Robust materials
    • Small speaker for acoustic indications
    • 100 led lights for light indications/effects
    • Battery not included.

    BOMBA 1S

    ROBUST, SIMPLE, PRACTICAL and ECONOMIC. Designed for all those who need a (simulated) bomb device that can withstand all kinds of blows, that does not require extensive explanations in the briefing, and that really wants to invest only for what they really need.

    • Steel encapsulation
    • Large screen with 265 high-luminosity LEDs.
    • Long duration push button (microswitch)
    • Simple operation through "mini-games". Intended for the player to discover how to arm or disarm the bomb without any explanation.
    • Ignition contact by key.
    • It only needs to be configured once (non-volatile memory). Countdown time setting and arm/disarm difficulty level.
    • High level alarm sound.
